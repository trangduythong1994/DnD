High fantasy character concept art of a [CREATURE] — a [brief identity / role].

Appearance: [body type, defining features, materials, signature traits].

Equipment / Attributes: [weapons, items, magical objects if any].

Action / Aura: subtle [magic / energy / presence], hinting at its nature without active combat.

Composition: single character, full body, centered, eye-level view, dominant subject, subject occupies ~75–85% of frame, clear silhouette.

Style: high fantasy, smooth shading, soft edges, painterly rendering, balanced colors, soft lighting, clean presentation.

Background: minimal or softly blurred environment, low detail, non-distracting, secondary to the subject.

Tone: [CREATURE TYPE] + [CR TIER]
Color palette: [matching palette]
